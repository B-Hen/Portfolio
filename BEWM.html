Blue Eyes White Magician

Blue Eyes White Magician is a top down shoot'em up game. The user plays as a wizard who must defeat trolls to gain money and explore different parts of the map to find power ups and more!. This game was made during the MAGIC game jam in a group of 3. I had the role of porgrammer. I helped to code movement and collision. I also made the win and lose conditions as well as coded the different games states. THis game was written in C# and made in Unity and was part of my very first game jam!.

Programming languages and Tools:

  • C#
  • Unity
  • Visual Studio 2019
